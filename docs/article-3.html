Learn Sub-Zero!

By GAMERSUPPS SPONSORED - Nov 20, 2023

Sub-Zero Playstyle

Sub-Zero, the cryomancer extraordinaire, is a formidable character in Mortal Kombat 11, known for his freezing abilities and deadly combos. To master the art of playing as Sub-Zero, understanding his unique moves and tactics is essential. Sub-Zero's primary strength lies in his ability to control the battlefield with freezing attacks, leaving opponents vulnerable to punishing combos.

One of Sub-Zero's signature moves is the Ice Ball, a freezing projectile that can stop opponents in their tracks. Utilize the Ice Ball strategically to create openings for powerful follow-up attacks. Additionally, Sub-Zero's Slide attack is an excellent tool for closing the distance quickly and catching opponents off guard. Mastering these fundamental moves is crucial for initiating combos and controlling the pace of the match.

Freezing Combos and Brutal Finishers

Sub-Zero's strength shines when executing freezing combos that transition seamlessly from one move to the next. Practice chaining together his special moves with standard attacks to create devastating combinations. His Fatal Blow, known as the 'Ice-Cutioner,' is a deadly finishing move that can turn the tide of a battle when executed strategically. Timing is key, and catching opponents off guard with a well-timed Fatal Blow can secure victory.

As a master of ice, Sub-Zero boasts several brutal finishing moves, known as Fatalities. These finishing sequences are not only visually stunning but also a testament to the character's ruthless efficiency. Experiment with Sub-Zero's Fatalities to discover the most satisfying and stylish ways to conclude your victories. Remember, executing Fatalities is not just about winning; it's about asserting dominance over your opponent in the most spectacular fashion.

In the hands of a skilled player, Sub-Zero becomes a freezing force to be reckoned with in Mortal Kombat 11. Whether you prefer controlling the pace of the match with freezing projectiles or delivering bone-chilling combos up close, Sub-Zero offers a diverse and rewarding playstyle for those who embrace the art of cryomancy.
